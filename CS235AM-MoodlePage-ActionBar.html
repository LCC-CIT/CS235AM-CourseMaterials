    Welcome to Android App Development!

    Start Here:

    Some of you are taking the online section of this course and others are in the on-campus section, either way you will use this same Moodle page. Regardless of the section you are in, you are welcome to either: a) come to class, b) participate in a live stream using Collaborate, or c) watch a Collaborate recording. There will be links in each week’s Moodle module for the live streams and recordings.

    You must have access to a computer that can run Visual Studio and have a good internet connection to participate in this class. And, in order to be successful, you must devote at least 12 hours a week to learning activities. This includes 4 hours participating in class (or Collaborate live streams, or recordings), and at least 8 hours studying and working on your own.

    Next, read the Getting Started online book, and then lets have fun writing apps!
