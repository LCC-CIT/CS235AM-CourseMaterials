CS235AM Potential Quiz 1 questions

Based on http://developer.xamarin.com/guides/cross-platform/getting_started/introduction_to_mobile_development/

What languages are used to do native Android and iOS development?
Java and Objective C or Swift, respectively

What development machine is required for iOS development? 
A Mac with OS-X Lion or above

What development machine is required for Android development?
Either a Mac with OS-X Lion (or above), or a PC with Windows Visa (or above)

When a Xamarin application is compiled, what is the output?
An application package that can be uploaded to an app store and used to install an app on a device. (.apk for Android and .app for iOS)
